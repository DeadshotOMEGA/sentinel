# Visitor Sign-In

> Audience: Duty Day Staff (DDS), watch supervisors

## Purpose

Capture visitor attendance accurately and maintain a traceable sign-in record.

## Procedure

1. Confirm visitor identity and authorization context.
2. Enter visitor details in sign-in flow.
3. Verify successful sign-in state.
4. Confirm visitor appears in operational presence tracking.
5. Escalate unusual access requests before completion.
