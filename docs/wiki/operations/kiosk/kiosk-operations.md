# Kiosk Operations

> Audience: Duty Day Staff (DDS), supervisors, kiosk operators

## Purpose

Operate the kiosk check-in station safely and consistently during high-frequency attendance workflows.

## Preconditions

- Kiosk station is online and reachable.
- Badge scanner is connected and unobstructed.
- Operator account is active for oversight.

## Operational Sequence

1. Confirm kiosk mode and station readiness.
2. Monitor badge scan results and invalid attempts.
3. Escalate repeated failures to badge administration process.
4. Verify presence updates after critical check-ins.
